## 1. Introduction

Welcome to the **QuickJobs Portal User Manual**. This comprehensive guide is designed to help you navigate and utilize the features of the QuickJobs Job Portal effectively. Whether you are a **Job Seeker** looking for your next career opportunity, an **Employer** seeking top talent, or an **Administrator** managing the platform, this guide covers every aspect of the system.

The QuickJobs Portal allows for seamless interaction between companies and candidates, offering advanced tools like a built-in Resume Builder, real-time application tracking, and detailed analytics.

---

## 2. Getting Started

### System Requirements
The QuickJobs Portal is a web-based application accessible from any modern device.
- **Browser:** Google Chrome (recommended), Mozilla Firefox, Safari, or Microsoft Edge.
- **Device:** Desktop, Laptop, Tablet, or Smartphone.
- **Internet Connection:** Stable broadband or mobile data connection.

### Accessing the Portal
1. Open your web browser.
2. Navigate to the portal URL (e.g., www.quickjobsportal.com).
3. You will land on the **Home Page**, where you can immediately start searching for jobs or log in to your account.

## 3. Account Management

### Registration
To access the full features of the portal, you must create an account. The portal supports two main user roles during signup:

**For Job Seekers:**
1. Click the **Sign Up** button on the top right.
2. Select **Job Seeker**.
3. Enter your **Full Name**, **Email Address**, and create a strong **Password**.
4. (Optional) You may be asked to provide initial skills or experience details.
5. Click **Register**.

**For Employers:**
1. Click the **Sign Up** button on the top right.
2. Select **Employer**.
3. Enter your **Company Name**, **Email Address**, and **Password**.
4. Provide details such as **Industry Type** and **Company Size**.
5. Click **Register**.

### Social Media Login
You can also sign up or log in using your Google or social media accounts for faster access.
1. On the Login/Signup page, look for the "Continue with Google" or similar buttons.
2. Follow the on-screen prompts to authorize the application.

### Email Verification
After registration, you may need to verify your email to activate your account.
1. Check your email inbox for a verification code (OTP) from QuickJobs.
2. Enter the OTP in the verification screen on the portal.
3. Click **Verify**.

### Logging In
1. Click **Login** on the homepage.
2. Enter your registered **Email** and **Password**.
3. Click the **Login** button.

### Password Recovery
If you forget your password:
1. Click the **Forgot Password?** link on the login page.
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. You will receive a password reset link or OTP.
4. Follow the instructions to set a new password.

## 4. For Job Seekers

### Job Seeker Dashboard
Upon logging in, you are greeted by the User Dashboard. This is your command center.
- **Profile Summary:** View your profile completion status.
- **Application Stats:** See total applications, pending reviews, and interview offers at a glance.
- **Recent Activity:** Quick links to recently viewed or applied jobs.

### Creating a Profile
A complete profile increases your chances of being hired.
1. Navigate to **Profile** via the user menu.
2. **Personal Info:** Upload a professional profile picture.
3. **Resume:** Upload your existing resume (PDF format) or use the Resume Builder.
4. **Skills & Experience:** Add your key skills (e.g., "Python", "Marketing"), qualification details, and past work experience.
5. Click **Save Changes**.

### Resume Builder
QuickJobs features an integrated Resume Builder to help you create professional CVs.
1. Go to the **Resume** section in the menu.
2. Choose a template:
   - **White Template:** Clean, sales/business-oriented.
   - **Green Template:** Simple and modern.
   - **Blue Template:** Professional and corporate.
3. Fill in the fields for Education, Experience, and Skills. The preview updates in real-time.
4. **Export** or **Save** your resume to your profile.

### Searching for Jobs
1. Click on **Jobs** in the main navigation.
2. Use the search bar to enter keywords (e.g., "Software Engineer").
3. **Filters:** Use the sidebar to refine by:
   - **Location:** City or Remote.
   - **Type:** Full-time, Part-time, Contract, Hourly.
   - **Level:** Internship, Fresher, Mid Level, Senior.
4. Browse the list. Trending and new jobs are often highlighted.

### Applying for Jobs
1. Click on a job card to view the **Job Details** page.
2. Read the description, requirements, and salary info.
3. Click the **Apply Now** button.
4. You will be prompted to:
   - Confirm your contact details.
   - Attach your Resume (or select the one from your profile).
   - Write a **Cover Letter** (optional but recommended).
5. Submit the application. You will receive a success message.

### Managing Applications
Track the status of your applications in real-time.
1. Go to **Dashboard > Applied Jobs**.
2. You will see a list of all jobs you've applied to.
3. **Status Indicators:**
   - **Pending:** The employer hasn't seen it yet.
   - **Reviewed:** The employer has opened your application.
   - **Shortlisted/Accepted:** Good news! Expect contact.
   - **Rejected:** The employer has decided not to proceed.

### Saved Jobs
Not ready to apply? Save jobs for later.
1. Click the **Heart Icon** (Save) on any job listing.
2. Access these later under **Dashboard > Saved Jobs**.

## 5. For Employers

### Company Profile Setup
Before posting jobs, ensure your company brand is attractive.
1. Go to **Employer Dashboard > Profile**.
2. Upload your **Company Logo**.
3. Fill in details: **Industry**, **Company Size**, **description**, **Address**, and **Website**.
4. Verify your account if required (Submit PAN/Tax documents if prompted by Admin).

### Employer Dashboard & Insights
The dashboard gives you a high-level view of your recruitment funnel.
- **Overview:** Total active jobs, total applicants, and new messages.
- **Insights:** View analytics such as job view counts, applicant demographics, or peak application times.

### Posting a Job
1. Click **Post a Job** in the navigation or dashboard.
2. Fill in the **Job Details**:
   - **Title**: e.g., "Senior React Developer".
   - **Category**: Select from the dropdown (e.g., IT, Finance).
   - **Type**: Full-time, Contract, etc.
   - **Salary Range**: Be transparent to attract better candidates.
   - **Deadine**: When the application window closes.
   - **Openings**: Number of vacancies.
3. **Description**: Use the rich text editor to list responsibilities and requirements.
4. Click **Publish**. The job is now live (subject to admin approval if configured).

### Managing Job Listings
1. Navigate to **Job List**.
2. You can view all your Active, Closed, or Draft jobs.
3. **Actions**:
   - **Edit**: Update job details.
   - **Close**: Stop receiving applications (e.g., if position is filled).
   - **Delete**: Remove the listing entirely.

### Reviewing Applicants
1. Go to **Applicants** or click "View Applicants" on a specific job.
2. You will see a list of candidates.
3. Click a candidate to view their **Profile, Resume, and Cover Letter**.
4. **Take Action**:
   - **Status Update**: Change status from "Pending" to "Reviewed", "Accepted", or "Rejected".
   - **Download Resume**: Get their PDF for offline review.

## 6. For Administrators

### Admin Dashboard
Admins have a "God-view" of the platform.
- **Stats:** Total Users, Total Employers, Active Jobs, Total Application volume.
- **Recent Activity:** monitor new signups and job posts.

### User & Employer Management
1. **Users:** Go to **User Management** to search, view, or ban specific job seekers.
2. **Employers:** Go to **Employer Management**.
   - **Verification:** Review documents from new employers and mark them as "Verified" to give them a trust badge.

### Job Verification & Oversight
Admins ensure the quality of listings.
1. Go to **Job Management**.
2. Review reported jobs or new high-priority listings.
3. You can edit categories under **Job Category Management** to keep the taxonomy clean (e.g., adding "AI/ML" category).

### Revenue Management
(If applicable)
1. Go to **Revenue** section.
2. View financial reports if the platform charges employers for premium postings or featured listings.

## 7. Troubleshooting & Support

### Common Issues
- **"I cannot login"**: Ensure your Caps Lock is off and you are using the correct email. Try the "Forgot Password" function.
- **"Upload failed"**: Ensure your Resume is a PDF and under 5MB. Profile pictures should be JPG/PNG.
- **"My job isn't visible"**: Check if the job status is "Active". If you are a new employer, your account might still be pending verification.
